## How to sign

You don't need to print or email anything. When you open your first pull request,
an automated check will ask you to confirm this Agreement by leaving a single
comment on the PR:

> **I have read the CLA Document and I hereby sign the CLA**

That signature is recorded once and covers all your future contributions. If you
are contributing on behalf of a company, please contact the maintainer for a
corporate CLA before your team submits PRs.
